Travel Guide

Getting Here

How to reach the province from main transport hubs.

The Main Route

Because Dinagat Islands has no commercial airport, almost all travel routes through Surigao City first.

1. Travel to Surigao City

Surigao City in mainland Mindanao serves as the primary jump-off point to the islands.

By Air

Fly from Manila or Cebu directly to Surigao Airport (SUG). Flights are limited, so book early.

By Land / Sea (from nearby islands)

Buses from major Mindanao cities travel to Surigao. Ferries also run from Cebu to Surigao daily.

2. Ferry to San Jose, Dinagat

From the Surigao City Boulevard port, you'll need to catch a passenger boat or RORO (Roll-on/Roll-off) vessel.

Schedule & Duration

The trip takes about 1.5 to 2 hours depending on the vessel. Boats typically start running at 5:00 AM until around 3:00 PM.

Arrival Port

Ensure you take the boat bound for San Jose (the capital). The port is right at the town center.
