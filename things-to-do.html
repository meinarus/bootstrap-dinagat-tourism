Experiences

Things To Do

How to spend your days around the province.

Island Hopping

The primary activity in Dinagat. Rent a local outrigger boat to visit multiple beaches, coves, and rock formations in a single day.

Swimming & Snorkeling

The waters around the islands are notoriously clear. Bring your own snorkeling gear as rentals might not always be available.

Road Tripping

Hire a habal-habal to take you through the winding coastal roads of the main island for elevated views and local town visits.

Plan Your Days

Suggested Itineraries

Weekend Trip

2 Days, 1 Night

Day 1: Island Hopping

Arrive in San Jose. Rent a boat directly for Pangabangan Blue Lagoon, Bitaog Beach, and Duyos Beach.

Day 2: Mainland Spots

Rent a habal-habal. Visit the PBMA Shrine, view Islander's Castle, and depart back to Surigao.

Full Experience

3 Days, 2 Nights

Day 1: Arrival & Town

Arrive in San Jose. Settle into accommodation, explore the PBMA Shrine and town center.

Day 2: The Grand Boat Tour

Full day island hopping: Lake Bababu hike, Pangabangan Lagoon, Hagakhak Rock Formation, and nearby beaches.

Day 3: Coastal Ride

Habal-habal ride to northern coastal viewpoints before catching the afternoon ferry back.
